About Us

India is an agricultural land and farming provides the means of livelihood for the major Indian population. The Indian Economy is somehow also depends on agriculture. To be a part of the Incredible journey of Eco-Agric India, We provide best quality of services in the market like Compost, Seeds & Pesticide and to add-on all this we also provide best agriculture advice that helps farmers to increase their Productivity.

Services

Compost (Khad) Retailer

We are popular retailer to provides varities of Compost (Khad) with Best Price in the market. we gives value to our customers and also provides home delivery service to our trusted customers.

Seed (Beej)

We offers the best quality of Hybrid & Organic seeds for all kind of Vegetables, Paddy, Wheat, Mustard, etc. we also sell the Hybrid quality of Mentha plant.

Pesticide (Keetanaashak)

Pesticides help farmers to produce more with less land it also help to reduce waterborne and insect transmitted diseases for these we sell the original quality and registered Pesticides.

Agri-advisory (Salaah)

We Provides the best service to farmers and assist to develop their farming and management skills.
we also meet our costomers personaly to gives proper tips for farming.
